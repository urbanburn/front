An event like nothing Stockholm has ever seen… For one weekend, we build another universe together. No passive consumers, only active participants. Urban Burn is a participatory art event inspired by Burning Man. It’s an experiment in creative freedom & participation, conceived, built, experienced and returned to nothing by you! Urban Burn is a non-profit event. We are renting 20 000 sqm of open indoor space at Nobelberget in Sickla from March 11 to 13, where everyone is invited to create anything they like.

Get a ticket
You’ll need a ticket to participate in Urban Burn, which costs XXX SEK. You can buy it here. The money gathered for tickets is used for event expenses and project grants. We’re all creating this event on a voluntary basis, nobody gets paid.
Find or form a camp

Do you want to be part of this? To get a feeling what’s up, we invite you to take a look at some of the projects already planned. You can either join one of those projects, or start your own. Some of us are supervising placement, so in case you start a project, it would be great to send a message to XXX and explain what kind of space and other requirements (such as electricity) you need.

Project grants
Part of the money gathered for tickets is used to support you doing your project, in the form of a grant. Grants cover material expenses only. This page has all the information you need on how grants work, and how to apply for one.
